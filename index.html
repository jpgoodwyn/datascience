My Specialties

My favorite areas... and I'm pretty good at them too!

Python

1.5+ years coding in Python. I use Python the most lately. It's flexible, easy to learn, easy to teach, and has a package for almost any endeavor.

R

4+ years coding in R. While R is more specific-use than Python and a bit more difficult to learn, I love visualization in R with ggplot and reporting with R Markdown. My GUI of choice is unsurprisingly RStudio.

Visualization

Finding the story in data and realizing it as a visual is one of my favorite parts of data science. There are so many different types of charts and graphs to use, and I feel each has its own strengths in different situations (except maybe pie charts...)

User Experience Design

Whether it's requirements gathering or user interviews, I love making things easy to understand and use.

Data Mining & Cleaning

Being a "data janitor" is sometimes seen as the less glamorous part of data science, but I personally enjoy it. It's true that it takes more than 80% of the time on a project.

Predictive Analytics

Random forest, gradient boosting, decision trees... all awesome.
